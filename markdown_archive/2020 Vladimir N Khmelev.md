# Ultrasonic tool for the realization of combined action during the drilling of extraterrestrial objects\n\n## Abstract\nThe article is devoted to the problem of the efficiency increase of ultrasonic drilling of hard materials for the studies of the soils of the planets, asteroids and other space objects. It is proposed the solution of the problem by the help of developed device, which is able to realize simultaneously with ultrasound the action by low-frequency macro-oscillations and rotational motion of the leading edge of the tool. The tests of the practical construction of the developed device showed the possibility of the speed increase of the ultrasonic drilling due to the additional low-frequency oscillations and pseudo-rotation in up to 1.5 times.\n\n## 1. Introduction\nLarge and small extraterrestrial objects, such as planets, comets, asteroids are the objects for the studies. As a rule the soil intake is carried out in the form of the cores taken from various depths by the drilling of hollow tubular toll. Traditional drilling methods are not practically applied due to high clamping forces, heating of the drilling zone, and high energy consumption. Ultrasonic drilling is considered an alternative as it does not require large pressing forces and excludes heating.\n\n## 2. Construction description\nTo increase the efficiency of the drilling of hard and brittle soils under extraterrestrial conditions, a construction was developed that combines ultrasonic drilling with shock low-frequency and rotational motion (pseudo-rotation). The tool includes a piezoelectric transducer generating oscillations at 20 kHz and a loading mass that provides low-frequency (20 Hz) high-amplitude vibrations.\n\n## 3.
